What We Do

We aim to destigmatize mental illness, homelessness, and substance abuse disorder through community work and learning. We also host volunteer events where members can assist underrepresented groups in the community.

01

Education

We host multiple speakers to educate students on the importance of inclusion and the issues facing underrepresented populations

02

Volunteering

Members have to ability to volunteer with our partner organizations such as WPIC and Sojourner House to assist members of the community

03

Destigmatization

We work to remove preset notions of disabled people, the homeless, and those with SUD through our community outreach
